How SecurePay Works

A step-by-step guide to secure escrow transactions that protect both buyers and sellers.

01

Create Your Deal

Start by creating a new escrow deal. Specify the transaction details including amount, description of goods or services, and invite the counterparty to participate.

  • Define transaction terms clearly
  • Set deadlines and milestones
  • Invite buyer or seller
  • Both parties review and agree
02

Buyer Deposits Funds

Once both parties agree to terms, the buyer securely deposits the payment into our protected escrow account. Funds are held safely until delivery is confirmed.

  • Multiple payment options available
  • Bank-level encryption
  • Instant deposit confirmation
  • Seller notified immediately
03

Seller Delivers

With funds secured, the seller ships goods or provides services. Upload tracking numbers, delivery confirmation, or completion proof to the platform.

  • Upload delivery documentation
  • Real-time tracking updates
  • Buyer receives notifications
  • Secure document storage
04

Buyer Inspects

The buyer has a predetermined inspection period to verify goods or services. Test the product, review the work, and confirm everything meets expectations.

  • Configurable inspection period
  • Request modifications if needed
  • Communication portal built-in
  • Automatic reminders
05

Funds Released

Upon buyer confirmation or after the inspection period ends, funds are automatically released to the seller's wallet for withdrawal or future transactions.

  • Instant wallet transfer
  • Multiple withdrawal options
  • Transaction receipt generated
  • Complete audit trail

Built-In Protections

Every transaction includes multiple layers of security and protection.

Buyer Protection

If seller fails to deliver or goods don't match description, funds are returned to buyer automatically.

Seller Protection

Once buyer confirms delivery, funds are guaranteed. No chargebacks or payment reversals.

Fair Arbitration

If disputes arise, our professional team reviews evidence and makes impartial decisions.

Auto-Release

If buyer doesn't respond within inspection period, funds auto-release to prevent delays.

Secure Storage

Funds held in segregated FDIC-insured accounts, never commingled with operating funds.

Digital Agreements

Terms are recorded and legally binding. Both parties have access to complete documentation.

Dispute Resolution Process

If issues arise, our streamlined process ensures fair outcomes.

1

Dispute Raised

Either party can raise a dispute within the inspection period with reason and evidence.

2

Evidence Collection

Both parties submit documentation, communications, and supporting evidence.

3

Arbitration Review

Our professional team reviews all evidence within 5-7 business days.

4

Fair Decision

Decision made with detailed explanation. Appeal process available if needed.
