Quick Questions

Common questions answered before you reach out.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Email inquiries: Within 24 hours. Live chat: Under 2 minutes average. Phone: Immediate during business hours. Priority support available for Business and Enterprise plans.

Can I schedule a call with support?

Yes! Email us or use the live chat to schedule a callback at your convenience. We offer calls Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM PST. Enterprise customers have access to dedicated phone support.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your account email, deal ID (if applicable), and a detailed description of your issue. Screenshots and documents help us resolve issues faster.

Do you offer support in other languages?

Yes, we provide support in English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, and Portuguese. For other languages, we use professional translation services.
