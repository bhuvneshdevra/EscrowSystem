Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SecurePay Escrow services.

How does the escrow process work?

The buyer deposits funds into our secure escrow account. The seller is notified and ships the goods or provides the service. Once the buyer confirms receipt and satisfaction, funds are automatically released to the seller. If any issues arise, our dispute resolution team steps in.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers (ACH, Wire), credit/debit cards, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payment methods are processed through PCI-DSS compliant systems with bank-level encryption.

How long does fund release take?

Upon buyer confirmation, funds are released instantly to your SecurePay wallet. Bank transfers for withdrawal typically take 1-3 business days depending on your bank. Express withdrawal options are available for Business and Enterprise plans.

What happens in a dispute?

Our professional arbitration team reviews all evidence from both parties within 5-7 business days. We examine delivery confirmations, communications, and other documentation. Either party can appeal decisions, and cases with complex outcomes may be escalated to third-party arbitration.

Is my money safe?

Absolutely. All funds are held in segregated, FDIC-insured accounts. We use AES-256 encryption, multi-factor authentication, and undergo annual security audits. SecurePay is also SOC 2 Type II certified and PCI-DSS compliant.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. You only pay the transparent transaction fee based on your plan, or the monthly subscription for Business/Enterprise plans. Wire transfer fees may apply from your bank, but we don't charge for deposits or standard withdrawals.
